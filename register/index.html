Code of Conduct

Meetup Mixup Melbourne is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of meetup participants in any form. Meetup participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the meetup without a refund at the discretion of the event organizers.

A contact number will be publicised at the venue, should anyone need assistance.
