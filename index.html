Let's come together!

It's that time of year, when meetups unite the communities of Melbourne, and celebrate the end of the year together.

Register Today

We're thrilled to announce Melbourne's combined end of year mixer: Meetup Mixup Melbourne! It's a fantastic opportunity to relax with a cold beverage and celebrate a year of meetups!

The event is open to all, and we'll be gathering on December 21st for an evening of celebrations to round out the year and welcome the holiday season. Get in quick, as spaces are limited!

There's a $8.80 cover fee, which includes a drink and nibbles.

Code of Conduct

Meetup Mixup Melbourne is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of meetup participants in any form. Meetup participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the meetup without a refund at the discretion of the event organizers.

A contact number will be publicised at the venue, should anyone need assistance.
